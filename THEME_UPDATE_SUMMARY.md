1. **Pure Black Background (#000000)**

   - OLED-friendly (battery saving)

   - Modern fintech aesthetic
   - Friendly and approachable
   - Distinctive brand identity

4. **High Border Radius (28px)**
   - Premium feel
   - Soft and modern
   - Consistent with fintech apps

5. **Flat Design (No Shadows)**
   - Clean and minimal
   - Modern aesthetic
   - Reduces visual noise
